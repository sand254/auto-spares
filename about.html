About Us

HOME / About Us

From its humble beginnings in 2005, Auto Spares has grown up to be one of the most reputable Motor Vehicle Workshops in Nairobi County with proven track records in Motor Vehicle and Machinery repairs.

We are located within the Industrial Area of Nairobi, just at the entrance point of Nairobi East Industrial Park. We started off as a small-scale garage operating under different management. In 2010, the business was restructured and registered as a limited company by the name Auto Spares Ltd. In the year 2015, we converted to Permanently under the name Auto Spares, the name we still use to date.

Our philosophy is simple, regardless of what the job may be - whether it is vehicle major service, repairs, Normal service, panel beating or spray painting - we continuously strive to provide our clients with top class services.
